# JNM reviewer-boundary audit (2026-06-13)

This audit protects the active Journal of Nuclear Materials manuscript from
claim drift. Its purpose is not to add new scientific results, but to ensure
that every high-risk reviewer concern remains bounded by the current evidence.

## Required conservative phrases in the active manuscript

- `current calibration candidate`
- `not a final Li4SiO4 material law`
- `event-sequence evidence`
- `not as a converged fracture probability`
- `larger stochastic ensembles`
- `no coupled thermal-flow prediction`
- `not a measured material stiffness`
- `not to report a bulk elastic modulus`
- `repository DOI/URL to be added`

## Prohibited unqualified claims

The active manuscript must not make unqualified claims of:

- converged failure probability
- universal onset displacement
- lifetime prediction
- design-margin prediction
- final Li4SiO4 constitutive law
- fully quasi-static bed compression
- coupled thermal-flow or purge-flow prediction
- measured 1.5e10 Pa Li4SiO4 stiffness

Negated or boundary-setting uses are acceptable, for example "not as a
converged fracture probability" or "not a final Li4SiO4 material law".
